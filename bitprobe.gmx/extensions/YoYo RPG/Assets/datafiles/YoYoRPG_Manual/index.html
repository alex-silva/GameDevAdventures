Introduction

The YoYo Games RPG is a complete and customisable action RPG engine. The engine has all the things you would expect from this type of game: inventory, dialogues, shops, quests, etc... and all of them can be customised easily by you to create your own adventures.

Before looking at the code base, it is recommended that you play the game and explore the demo levels available, as that way you will have a better grasp of what the engine is capable of and what things you will want to change or tweak to suit your needs. To play the demo game that has been made to show off the engine features, click the green "Test Play" icon at the top of the GameMaker: Studio IDE, and you can find the game controls from the Getting Started link in the index below.

We hope you enjoy using this engine and if you have any questions, bugs or issues then please contact the YoYo Games HelpDesk here: http://help.yoyogames.com.
